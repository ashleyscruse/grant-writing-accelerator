# Your AI workspace

This is the workspace we use during the AI demo: a ready-to-go space where the AI tools and your professional context are wired together, so the AI drafts from your real record instead of guessing. Here is everything you need, in one place.

## What we do in the workshop

1. Open the workspace folder in **Claude Code** and say **hi**. It reads its own setup and becomes **Sage**, your guide.
2. **Build your profile:** drop your CV in the profile folder and tell Sage *"build my profile."* It reads your CV and asks a couple of things a CV can't answer.
3. **Put it to work:** paste a real funding opportunity and ask *"should I pursue this?"*, then draft a section, live.
